About Me


My name is Sara Roberts. I've worked in Professional Services in the SAAS industry for the past 5 years.

In my current role as a Managed Services Consultant at Softdocs, I partner with K-12 and higher education clients to develop E-Form business process solutions. I gather form requirements from stakeholders, design and code E-Forms in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, pull and push data to SQL tables and school ERP systems through integrations, create workflows using Softdoc's Etrieve product, and test end-to-end functionality of my solution.

I also have experience as a Lead Program Coordinator for a Digital Media Software corporation, where I led an onboarding program and drove implementation engagements for enterprise-level customers (product migrations, deployments, troubleshooting, etc). Additional responsibilities included establishing annual KPIs, coaching a team, and reporting program metrics to client stakeholders.

I am Scrum Master, Scrum Product Owner, and SAFe Agile certified and experienced in agile methodology, quality assurance and software development of web-based applications.

I received my first Bachelor of Science from Louisiana State University and my second Bachelors from Southeastern Louisiana University in Computer Science. I gained experience programming in Java, SQL, and JavaScript, and created a Web Application in React/.NET as my capstone that is still used by the client today. I also have several years’ experience as a manual QA Tester for EA Games.

As a consultant, I consider myself to be someone genuine who brings creative and complete solutions to client problems. I value independence, transparency, and honesty at work. I'm driven by delivering what clients ask for, and more.

I've also mentored new team members as a Career Guide, contributed as part of the Culture and Philanthropy committee, written company blogs and sales memos, won an Extra Mile award for several quarters, and always participate in extra curricular work activities.

I have two calico cats (Mae and Rue) and I enjoy watercoloring, arts and crafts, watching true crime, and karaoke.
